About Us

The first of Pat's "Salmon Cookies" bakeries opened on October 8th, 2015 at the now-famous 1st & Pike location. After one too many complaints from his children about wanting dessert before dinner, founder Pat Jobezogates turned his passion for salmon and his family's love for cookies into the ultimate combination for lovers of seafood and sweets. Now, with bakeries throughout the Seattle area, and new locations emerging, "Salmon Cookies" is the future of Pacific Northwest confections!
